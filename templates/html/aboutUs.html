About us

Chunk IT is a platform that enables authenticated user to upload large CSV and JSON files and break them into smaller CHUNKS after the users have uploaded. This platform also allows users upload and chunk other file formats such as HTML, XLS and HTM. We offer our users secure access and at the conclusion of each conversion, you have the option of deleting the document yourself. We won't copy, inspect, or otherwise examine your files. We make sure users can effortlessly and securely convert.
